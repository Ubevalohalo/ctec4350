About

This one-day conference serves as an engaging platform to bring together nonprofit leaders, UTA faculty, and students for an immersive exploration of the latest trends, strategies, and tools shaping fundraising & development today. Through engaging plenary sessions, hands-on workshops, and meaningful networking opportunities, participants will gain actionable insights and innovative approaches to strengthen their impact. By fostering collaboration and dialogue across sectors, this event seeks to inspire new partnerships and empower attendees with the resources and connections needed to address our community's most pressing challenges.
